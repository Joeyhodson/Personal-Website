 Fishing / Spearfishing

This may be one my most treasured projects that I have completed since this was my first ever side-project related to my field. This project wasn't necessarily difficult and may not be extremely intriguing to the naked eye, however, at the time, it was to me. The LED Cube opened a whole new world of electronics to me and really inspired me to continue to work with microcontrollers. I was completely mindblown to the fact that I had created the flashing cube before me.
