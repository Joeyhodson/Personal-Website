 3D Printing

This is a relatively new hobby of mine but I have been interested in 3D printing since the emergence of printers during my tenure in high school. After printers became affordable, I purchased one, a Prusa Mini. I figured it was a reasonable purchase since I could use it for a lot of my electronics projects. I cannot exemplify the amount of times in which I needed a small box to house my electronics for a project. Not only have I used my printer for my projects, but I have created relatively trivial objects that have either replaced broken objects or were simply just handy. I plan to create desk pieces as well!
