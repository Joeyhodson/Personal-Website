 Fishing / Spearfishing

Having grown up on the coast of Florida with a father who owned a boat, fishing came naturally to me as a way of life. From my earliest memories, I was off fishing in the Eggmont Channel right out of Tampa Bay. Only later on in my childhood did my father, brothers, and I start to spearfish. In fact, the first time I scuba dived I was in St. Croix. My brothers and I soon became scuba certified allowing us to spearfish in our favorite places, the Florida Keys and back home in the Gulf of Mexico.
